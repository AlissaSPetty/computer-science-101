### Tree Traversals

How we print out a tree as a string 

* _In Order_ - Left, Root, Right

* _Preorder_ - Root, Left, Right

* _Postorder_ - Left, Right, Root

* _Level Order_ - Top to Bottom

Real World Examples:
  - Directories (file folders in a finder)
  - Database indexing
  - Decision Trees (machine learning or video games)

                        20
                    10      25
                  5   13  23
                    7       24
                  6   8

In Order: 5,6,7,8,10,13,20,23,24,25

Preorder: 20,10,5,7,6,8,13,25,23,24

Postorder: 6,8,7,5,13,10,24,23,25,20

Level Order: 20,10,25,5,14,23,7,24,6,6
